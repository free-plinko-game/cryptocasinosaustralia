### What is Litecoin?

Litecoin is a peer-to-peer cryptocurrency, established in 2011 by former Google engineer Charlie Lee. Often described as the silver to Bitcoin's gold, Litecoin was designed to provide quicker transaction verification, thus addressing some of the limitations associated with Bitcoin. It utilizes Scrypt as a proof-of-work algorithm, enabling faster block generation times, typically every 2.5 minutes as opposed to Bitcoin's 10 minutes.
