### Understanding VPNs in Online Gambling

VPNs work by encrypting a user's internet connection and routing it through a server in another location. This changes the user's apparent IP address, masking their physical location and providing an added layer of privacy. In the context of online gambling, VPNs allow players to access gambling sites that may otherwise be restricted in their country, enabling them to enjoy a wider array of gaming options.
