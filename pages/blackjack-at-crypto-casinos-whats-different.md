### Key Differences

#### Use of Cryptocurrency

One of the most significant differences when playing blackjack at a crypto casino is the use of cryptocurrencies for wagering, such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Litecoin. This method of betting offers several advantages, including increased privacy, faster transactions, and reduced or eliminated transaction fees. #### Provably Fair Gaming

Crypto casinos often employ a system called "provably fair" to ensure the fairness of their games. This system uses blockchain technology to provide transparent and verifiable proof of fair play, allowing players to confirm the integrity of each game round. In a provably fair blackjack game, algorithms can be used to generate random results that cannot be altered by the casino, which contrasts with traditional online casinos that typically rely on third-party auditing for fairness verification.
