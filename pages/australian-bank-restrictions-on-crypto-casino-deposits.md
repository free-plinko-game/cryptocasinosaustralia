- **Volatility and Speculation:** Cryptocurrencies are known for their price volatility, which can lead to significant financial losses.
- **Fraud and Scams:** The decentralized nature of cryptocurrencies makes them susceptible to fraudulent activities.
- **Legal Compliance:** Adhering to national and international regulations is paramount, and banks may restrict transactions that are difficult to monitor or trace.

Although specific bank policies regarding crypto casinos vary, several Australian banks have taken steps to either restrict or prohibit transactions involving these platforms. These actions are in part to reduce their exposure to risks and ensure adherence to legal requirements.
