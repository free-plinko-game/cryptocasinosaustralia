### Origins of Plinko

The game of Plinko was first introduced as a pricing game on the television game show "The Price Is Right" in 1983. Contestants would drop a disc from the top of a pegged board, with the disc bouncing off the pegs until it landed in one of several prize slots at the bottom. This seemingly straightforward format captured the audience's attention due to its randomness and the possibility of achieving significant rewards.

### Gameplay Mechanics

In crypto casinos, Plinko emulates the classic game but with modern features and bonuses. A typical Plinko game consists of a triangular-shaped board filled with pegs and slots at the bottom. Players select a position from which the disc begins its descent. As the disc collides with the pegs, it changes direction, adding an element of unpredictability to where the disc will eventually land.
