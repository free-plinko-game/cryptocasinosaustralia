### Ethereum and Smart Contracts

Ethereum is a decentralized platform that enables developers to build and deploy smart contracts—self-executing contracts with the terms of the agreement directly written into code. Smart contracts operate on the Ethereum blockchain, ensuring that transactions are secure, transparent, and free from third-party interference. These characteristics make smart contracts particularly appealing for online casinos.
