### Game Mechanics

In Keno, players select numbers, typically between one and 80, on a card. After selections are made, a set of numbers is drawn at random. Players win prizes based on how many of their chosen numbers match the numbers drawn by the casino. The more matches, the higher the payout.

Most crypto casinos adhere to these basic principles while offering variations designed to enhance the gaming experience. These variations may include altered payout structures, unique themes, and interactive graphics. Importantly, Keno in crypto casinos often features a Random Number Generator (RNG) to ensure fairness and unpredictability.
