### Overview of Bitcoin Cash

Bitcoin Cash was established in August 2017 as a hard fork from Bitcoin. This fork was mainly motivated by debates surrounding Bitcoin's scalability limitations. The primary technical change in Bitcoin Cash was the increase in block size, allowing more transactions to be processed faster and cheaper compared to Bitcoin. This increase in capacity has positioned Bitcoin Cash as an attractive alternative for online transactions, including within the gambling industry.
