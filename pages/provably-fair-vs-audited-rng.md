## Provably Fair vs Audited RNG

**Provably fair** and **audited Random Number Generators (RNGs)** are two systems used by online casinos, including those in the crypto sector, to ensure fairness and transparency in gaming. Both approaches aim to instill player confidence by demonstrating that game outcomes are unpredictable and unbiased. While they share a common purpose, the methods and technologies they employ are distinct, leading to different implications for players and operators.

### Provably Fair

The concept of "provably fair" is particularly relevant to crypto casinos. It is a mathematical approach that leverages cryptographic techniques to allow players to verify the fairness of each game outcome, providing a level of transparency not possible with traditional methods.

#### How Provably Fair Works

1. **Seed Generation**: Before a game begins, both the casino and the player provide a random seed. Typically, the casino's seed is a result of complex algorithms, while the player's seed is generated from client-side input.

2. **Hashing**: The casino seed is hashed into a cryptographic hash, which is shared with the player before the game begins, ensuring the casino cannot alter its seed retrospectively.

3. **Outcome Calculation**: The game's outcome is determined by combining both seeds, processed through a publicly known algorithm. This ensures that neither party can predict or manipulate the result ahead of time.

4. **Verification**: Players can check the fairness of each result by using the pre-shared hash, their input, and the game's algorithm. This verification ensures the outcome was not altered or predetermined.

#### Advantages of Provably Fair

- **Transparency**: Players can independently verify each outcome, ensuring the casino’s integrity.
- **Security**: The use of cryptographic methods secures the process against tampering.
- **Trust**: Since players can verify outcomes themselves, a provably fair system builds inherent trust between operator and user.

### Audited RNG

Audited RNGs, in contrast, rely on third-party verification of the randomness of game outcomes. Most traditional online casinos use this method, which involves certified testing and compliance with regulatory standards.

#### How Audited RNG Works

1. **RNG Implementation**: The online casino incorporates RNG software to produce random game outcomes.

2. **Third-Party Auditing**: Independent agencies audit the RNG software for randomness and fairness. These audits test the system against statistical standards to confirm that the RNG is functioning as intended.

3. **Certification**: After successful testing, the RNG receives certification from the auditing body, which the casino regularly renews to maintain trust and compliance with regulations.

#### Advantages of Audited RNG

- **Regulatory Compliance**: It meets industry regulations and standards, which varies by jurisdiction.
- **Reputation**: Trusted third-party auditors lend credibility to the casino’s operations.
- **Confidence for Players**: The involvement of recognizable auditing firms can reassure players unfamiliar with cryptography.

### Comparison

#### Transparency and Verification

- **Provably Fair** mechanisms offer direct player verification, creating a transparent gaming environment. On the other hand, audited RNG systems depend mainly on external audits that players must trust.

#### Technological Requirements

- Provably fair systems require a more sophisticated technological understanding from both the operator and the player due to their reliance on cryptographic processes. Conversely, audited RNGs leverage established regulatory frameworks and recognizable audit processes.

#### Popularity

- Provably fair systems are typically associated with crypto casinos, benefiting from the digital currency community's appreciation for transparency and technology. Audited RNG remains the standard in traditional online casinos due to its alignment with existing regulatory structures.

### Conclusion

Both provably fair and audited RNG systems serve vital roles in assuring game fairness in online gambling. Provably fair gaming appeals to players who value transparency and autonomy, whereas audited RNGs align with conventional regulatory practices and expectations. Players' choice between the two often depends on their individual preferences for verification and trust mechanisms.
